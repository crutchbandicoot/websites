About

We are in the pursuit of providing professional cleaning services to all our clients regardless of their size. We also want to ensure that in this pursuit our services are delivered with passion, honesty and dedication by all our staff members.

We are a 100% South African black owned and managed commercial cleaning and sanitation Service Company. Established in 2004, the company employs a team of over 190 staff members who are experienced and take pride in their work. Our entire workforce is professionally trained in their individual roles, motivated and reliable. Professionalism in their work, a personal service and determination to provide value for money service are the hallmark of the Company.

Our continued growth as a company has been achieved by having a set of systems in place. These systems include a detailed way in which we supervise our contracts, we carry out continuous inspections on sites we are contracted on, all of this this is combined with regular interactions with our clients and staff.

The founding memebers of Samagaba are commited and hands on in the company so that they can ensure that they can ensure an excellent service is given to our clients. This also helps in managing the organic growth of the company while the quality and standards are not compromised.
